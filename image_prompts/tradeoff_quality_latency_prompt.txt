-------------
Optional supplementary figure (not required by the current paper layout
but ready to drop in) for the IEEE SMC paper "Multi-Agent Retrieval as
an Alternative to Classical RAG". The figure visualizes the
quality/latency Pareto frontier across the four retrieval mechanisms
on two educational tutoring benchmarks.

FIGURE INTENT
-------------
- X axis: "Latency per example (ms)". Lower is better; place the
  preferred operating zone on the LEFT.
- Y axis: "Rubric coverage" (shared across both datasets and both a
  faithful proxy for the headline quality metric). Higher is better;
  preferred zone is at the TOP.
- Two dataset subplots side by side, identical axes:
  left subplot "TutorEval (n = 834, identical examples)",
  right subplot "EduBench (synchronized snapshot, n >= 1,580)".
- Four points per subplot, one per retrieval mechanism.
